# Project Idea Overview

# Problem Statement

Many individuals and businesses sign contracts without fully understanding them due to complex legal language. This can lead to hidden risks, financial loss, and unfair agreements.

# Proposed Solution

An AI-based Contract & Financial Intelligence Platform that helps users understand contracts, detect risks, and evaluate financial impact before signing.

# What the System Does

* Explains contracts in simple language
* Detects risky or unfair clauses
* Calculates financial impact of contract terms
* Identifies hidden charges and possible fraud
* Provides basic financial insights
* Supports multilingual explanations
* Allows users to ask questions using an AI chatbot

# Key Innovation

Unlike existing solutions, this system not only explains contracts but also analyzes their financial impact, helping users make better financial decisions.

# Target Users

* Individuals reviewing agreements
* Freelancers and professionals
* Startups and small businesses
* Organizations managing contracts

# Types of Contracts Targeted

The system focuses on analyzing commonly used contracts where financial and legal risks are high:

* Vendor Agreements (primary focus for business transactions)
* Loan Agreements (focus on financial obligations and penalties)
* SaaS Agreements (focus on hidden charges and subscription terms)
* Insurance Agreements (bonus category for complex policy analysis)

These contract types were selected based on their complexity and high impact on users’ financial decisions.
